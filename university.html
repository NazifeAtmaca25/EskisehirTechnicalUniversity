About Us

Established by Law No. 7141 published in the Official Gazette No. 30425 dated May 18, 2018, Eskişehir Technical University has 5 Faculties, 2 Vocational Schools, 1 College and 3 campuses in 3 campuses located in the center of Eskişehir, a city of science, culture and also youth. It consists of the Institute and 6 Research and Application Centers.

Eskişehir Teknik Üniversitesi, eğitim öğretim ve araştırma faaliyetlerini yürüttüğü güçlü altyapıya sahip birimlerinde, her biri alanındaki gelişmeleri takip eden ve tüm zamanını öğrencileriyle paylaşan yetkin öğretim kadrosuyla, öğrencilerini yaratıcı ve dinamik eğitim ortamlarında, geleceğe en donanımlı ve rekabet edebilir bireyler olarak hazırlamak üzere çalışmaktadır. Üniversitemiz 2 Eylül Yerleşkesinde bulunan uluslararası Hasan Polatkan Havalimanının işletmesi Havacılık ve Uzay Bilimleri Fakültemiz tarafından yürütülmektedir. Hasan Polatkan Havalimanına haftanın çeşitli günlerinde farklı havayolu şirketleri tarafından gerçekleştirilen uluslararası uçuşlar bulunmaktadır.


Rector

Prof. Dr. Adnan Özcan received his undergraduate degree in 1987, master's degree in 1990 and doctorate degree in 1997 from Anadolu University. He started his academic career at Anadolu and Osmangazi Universities and was a visiting researcher at Leeds University in England. He worked as an assistant professor at Anadolu University between 1997-2005. In 2005, he became an associate professor in Anadolu University, Faculty of Science, Department of Chemistry, Department of Analytical Chemistry, and received the title of professor in 2010.

Prof. Özcan served as Vice Rector responsible for Education, Quality Assurance Processes and International Relations at Anadolu University. He also served as vice rector at Eskişehir Technical University. His academic studies are generally on adsorption, biosorption, supercritical extraction, nano-sized adsorbent production and pollution removal. He has a total of 54 scientific articles, 43 papers, more than 100 referees and 11 national projects. Özcan trained 4 master's and 2 doctoral students. He has received various awards for his work in the field of pollution removal by dyeing and adsorption methods without using water in supercritical environments, one of which is the prestigious "Science and Technology Award" of Anadolu University. It has also won awards abroad.
